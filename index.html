About Your Doctor

Dr. Divya Agarwal — Compassionate Skin Care Expert

Dr. Divya Agarwal brings over 9 years of dedicated dermatological expertise to Dermaroots Skin & Aesthetiq Clinic in Indirapuram, Ghaziabad. Specialising in paediatric dermatology, she is renowned for her gentle, patient-first approach with young patients and their families.

Her areas of expertise span medical dermatology (eczema, psoriasis, vitiligo), cosmetic procedures (chemical peels, lasers, fillers) and comprehensive skin care for all age groups. She is committed to evidence-based treatments tailored to each patient's unique needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Dermaroots suitable for children? +

Absolutely. Dr. Divya Agarwal is a paediatric dermatologist with extensive experience in treating infants, children and teenagers for all skin conditions.

What should I bring to my first appointment? +

Bring previous prescriptions, allergy records, any skin photos, and avoid applying makeup or heavy creams to the affected area before visiting.

How many sessions of laser do I need? +

Typically 4–8 sessions depending on the condition. Dr. Agarwal will create a personalised plan during your initial consultation.

Is patient data kept confidential? +

Yes. We follow strict HIPAA and HITECH guidelines. All patient data is encrypted, securely stored and never shared without written consent.
